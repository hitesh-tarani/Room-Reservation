IIT Mandi Room Booking System(IMRBS) : FAQ



FAQ 1. What is IMRBS?


Ans. IMRBS is a system for the entire institute to view occupancy details of various venues (mainly classrooms) and offers a facility to authorized users to book a venue.


FAQ 2. Which rooms are currently being handled by IMRBS?


Ans. IMRBS will currently display the following groups, which will in turn have a list of rooms shown under them.

Lecture Rooms (120 & 50 seaters)

Conference Rooms (100 & 25 seaters)

PC-LABS (50 seaters) First Floor

Seminar Rooms(80 seaters)


IMRBS will show the current occupancy and additional booking details of the rooms that are listed under the above groups (areas).


FAQ 3. I do not see my course listed in the expected venue which come under one of the groups mentioned above. Why is it so?


Ans. IMRBS is super-imposing the entire institute/ department time-table in its occupancy chart. Hence if a venue is not shown as blocked for some course, it is because that the time-table entry for that course has not been recorded against the expected venue.


Please contact Academic office at acad@iitm.ac.in,ug_acad@iitm.ac.in giving details of the course and the correct venue that should be updated against it.


FAQ 4. Can I book a room in IMRBS?

[Question raised by a faculty/ office staff]


Ans. At the moment, bookings in IMRBS is only through Academic office. In case you wish to book any venue that you see in IRBS, please contact Academic office at acad@iitm.ac.in,ug_acad@iitm.ac.in mentioning the date, venue and duration details.
